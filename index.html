Contest Format

8 teams compete in a bracket-style tournament inspired by the NCAA March Madness format. Teams are seeded 1-8 and paired in quarterfinal matchups.

The bracket winner is determined by head-to-head matchup scores each round. The team winner is the team with the most total points across all rounds, regardless of bracket outcome.

Round 1 - Quarterfinals: 4 matchups (Mar 23-25) • Round 2 - Semifinals: 2 matchups (Mar 26-27) • Round 3 - Championship: 1 matchup (Mar 30-31)
